About
The Free Speech Movement (FSM) was a college campus protest that began in 1964 when several students at the University of California, Berkeley protested a ban on on-campus political activities, demanding their right to free speech and academic freedom. The FSM later ignited an unprecedented wave of student activism and involvement across the nation.
